Empowering Knowledge: Your Questions Answered

  • How long does it take to set up a company in the UAE?
    Most businesses can be registered within 3 to 10 working days, depending on the license type, business activity, and approvals required. Free zone companies are usually faster, while mainland and special activities may take slightly longer. Startup Finder handles the entire process and keeps you updated at every stage.
  • Do I need to be in the UAE to start my business?
    In many cases, no. We can start your company remotely using digital documents and power of attorney. You only need to visit the UAE for visa stamping, biometrics, or bank account finalization. Startup Finder guides you through everything step by step.
  • What is the total cost to start a company?
    The cost depends on your license type, visa requirements, office space, and approvals. There is no one fixed price, but Startup Finder provides clear, upfront quotations with no hidden fees, so you know exactly what you are paying for.
  • Will Startup Finder support me after company setup?
    Yes. We provide ongoing support including visa services, PRO services, license renewals, accounting, and compliance management. Our goal is to be your long-term business partner, not just a setup agent.
